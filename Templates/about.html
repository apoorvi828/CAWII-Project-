About Our Project

Unveiling the data to foster a safer India for women

Project Overview

Our Mission

The Women Safety Analytics Project aims to leverage data science and analytics to shed light on the critical issue of crimes against women in India. By analyzing comprehensive datasets, we seek to uncover patterns, trends, and insights that can inform policy decisions and drive societal change.

Our goal is to provide a data-driven foundation for initiatives aimed at enhancing women's safety and promoting gender equality across the nation.

Our Impact

Informing Policy

Our findings have been cited in parliamentary discussions and have influenced local policy decisions across multiple states.

Raising Awareness

Through media partnerships, we've reached millions of citizens, fostering a more informed public dialogue on women's safety.

Empowering NGOs

Our data and insights have equipped numerous NGOs with the information needed to target their efforts more effectively.
